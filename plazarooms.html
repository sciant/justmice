Meeting Rooms


Park Plaza London Riverbank An outstanding events venue, Park Plaza London Riverbank is AIM Gold accredited by the Meetings Industry Association. The hotel offers a choice of 23 function suites, including the large Ballroom accommodating up to 540 guests for dinner. Access is via a private conference entrance, enchanting spiral staircase and dedicated conference-level lift. The newly-built City Suites on the ground oor can accommodate up to 400 guests for reception. On the 12th oor, the Executive Boardroom offers stunning river views and can be hired for small private board meetings and dinners for up to 10 people. Larger conferences can benefit from a coach drop-off point and a designated group entrance. All events can be enhanced with state-of-the-art audiovisual technology.

Situated on the Mezzanine level, the private meeting rooms feature the latest technology, including drop-down projectors and screens, televisions, hardwiring for computers, as well as Wi-Fi. Contemporary and characterful, our two Private Dining Rooms provide possibilities for discreet dining or celebrations.
