Welcome to the Corinthia Hotel London...


Corinthia Hotel London is a five star hotel developed on the site of the former Metropole building which opened in 1885.
As London’s 21st Century Grand Hotel, we combine grace and grandeur with contemporary style and the most modern amenities. We have transformed an imposing grand Victorian building into a modern-day destination with luxurious guestrooms, exquisite dining and contemporary, characterful meeting space with advanced technology. Facing the River Thames, and a stone’s throw away from Trafalgar Square, Westminster and Whitehall, we are perfectly positioned in the heart of London for meetings and events ranging from a discreet business supper to a major international conference.

We would be delighted to welcome you!
